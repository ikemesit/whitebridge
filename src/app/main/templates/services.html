Our Services

Whitebridge Consulting seeks to bridge the manpower supply, efficiency & knowledge gaps in organizations with a view to helping organizations achieve set goals & objectives. Whitebridge specialises on Human Resource consulting which involves helping organisations attract, acquire, develop & retain qualified workforce in the right balance at all times. This it achieves through:

  • Human Resource Structuring/Restructuring
  • Corporate Learning & Development
  • Manpower Planning & Audit
  • Recruitment & Outsourcing

The company is positioned to render these services to small, medium & large scale organizations. The firm is a private limited liability company with corporate office in Uyo, Akwa Ibom state. Whitebridge Consulting is licensed & managed by experienced & chartered human resource professionals who are passionate about organizational development through appropriate application of Human Resource policies, strategies & programs.

Whitebridge consulting to become an internationally recognized Human Resource consulting firm through consistent & continuous personal & organisational development ventures. The whole aims & objectives of organizations are anchored on working with the right & motivated employees. Come on board, let's develop your people & grow your organization together. click any of our services below for more details:

HR Structuring / Restructuring

Entrepreneurs are usually only concerned with other factors of production & their business interest, ignoring the personnel aspect. They are mostly unconcerned about how people are sourced, managed & maintained for the survival & growth of the business.

Human resource structuring is the process of setting up structures & control measures for people management in an organization. It involves developing policies, procedures & processes that would be used to acquire, reward, maintain, retain & exit an organization’s most valuable asset – PEOPLE.

At Whitebridge, we help new & existing businesses develop structures that are individually applicable yet fit international best practices.

Recruitment & Placement Services

An organization is only as good as the personnel working therein; therefore the quality of personnel working in an organization determines its output & achievements. Knowing this, Whitebridge ensures that round pegs are put in round holes in every of its recruitment engagement. Recruitment refers to the process of attracting, screening, selecting, placement & on-boarding a qualified person for a job.

With proper job analysis & specification, we are sure to give to an organization only the best people to work for them & at competitive industry remuneration. We are aware that if we get it right at the recruitment level, every other function of human resources will run smoothly. It was Maurice McDonald, a co-founder of McDonald Corporation who dropped this timeless business fact, “you are only as good as the people you hire”. Let’s make you good with the right people.

Corporate Learning & Development

The practice of Human Resource is constantly evolving in line with trends & organizational demands. Hence, the function hitherto known as Training is now mostly referred to as Training & Development, & of recent, Learning & Development. This is because human resource practitioners don’t just seek to train employees but to ensure they learn & develop in line with their job functions or challenges.

As a Human Resource consulting firm, we will be rendering the service of learning & development to our client with the sole objective of adding value to their bottom line. We have respectable & experienced resource persons that are made up of seasoned professionals to deliver well-articulated learning & development demands in a most friendly & relaxed environment using modern & adequate facilities.

At Whitebridge, we do not just jump into Learning & Development contract with a client, we will be sure to conduct a Learning & Development Need Analysis to ensure vacuums are identified which our Learning & Development process will fill so as to be able to measure the Learning & Development impact on employees & organizations overtime.

Manpower Planning & Auditing

Failing to plan is planning to fail. As simple as that sounds, it applies to every facet of life. So any organization that does not plan its manpower is on a faulty foundation. Manpower Audit is a function that seeks to point out loopholes in practices & policies of HR & recommend suggestions for improvement. Whitebridge Consulting renders the Manpower Planning function to start-ups & established organizations, & renders the Manpower Audit service to large scale & public sector organizations. We realize the fact that an organization is only as effective as its workforce & as such, we will plan the manpower towards greater output for clients without compromising standards.

Employee Performance Management

Every organization has its set goals & objectives which they expect their employees to assist in meeting. Often times, these goals are not met & the organization may want to know the reason(s). Hence, Performance Management is a human resource function that seeks to continually appraise, monitor & improve employee’s performance in line with organizational goals. The needs for uninfluenced & unbiased opinion/report are some of the reasons why strategic firms outsource this function.

In undertaking this function, our focus is always on improved productivity & increased profitability with the aid of improved performance by employees. The growth of an organization depends largely on the efficiency of its employees.
