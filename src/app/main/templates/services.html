Our Services

Our Services

At Whitebridge, we focus on rendering various human resources services with the view to adding measurable value to organizations. Our team comprises of licensed and experienced human resource professionals who are passionate about organizational development through the appropriate application of human resource policies, strategies and programs

Employee Performance Management

An organization is successful when her people have the capacity and diligence to meet goals and objectives. We also understand that a times, there might be challenges faced by employees that lead to in-efficiency and poor performance. That’s why our Performance Management solution seeks to address this challenges.

At Whitebridge , we appraise, monitor and improve employee’s performance in line with  organizational goals. Our professional and results-oriented reports are some of the reasons why our services are sought after by organizations today.

In undertaking this function, we are highly focused on improved productivity and increased profitability delivered by competent employees. After all, the growth of an organization depends totally on the efficiency of its employees.

Recruitment & Placement Services

An organization is only as good as her people and largely translates to the quality of it’s outputs and achievements. As such, we’re in the business of identifying and recruiting competent professionals that will drive the success of your organization.

 

Through proper Job analysis and specification, we align organizations with the best people at a competitive industry remuneration. We are aware that if we get it right at the recruitment level, every other function of human resources will run smoothly.

We also specialize in outsourcing competent personnel of different cadre, skills and qualifications to assist organizations in achieving their aims and objectives. Our personnel are highly trained, motivated and experienced to hit the ground running in every sector.

Corporate Learning & Development

The practice of Human Resources is constantly evolving in line with trends and organisational demands. That’s why, White-bridge specialises in training employees and ensuring that they learn and develop the required skills needed for the modern day workplace and its challenges.

Our services are designed to add measurable value to our client’s bottom line. Our seasoned professionals are positioned to transfer knowledge and the know-how in a friendly environment using modern technologies and facilities.

In order to achieve the goals of empowering employees and their organizations alike, we conduct a learning and development analysis before offering our training solutions. This helps us understand the knowledge gaps that needs to bridged, the required expertise needed to create the much needed positive return and growth our clients cherish..
